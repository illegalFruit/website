Why Blog

I've always liked blogs. People's chosen representation of ideas online. The colors chosen, words, style, all of it. Some people want to be infortmative, other's personal, but all entertaining. And for years now I've thought about joining in too. But why? Not like I have an audience but that's fine. If only two or three friends enjoyed it, I think that's enough for me. Plus, it's like a blank canvas for my uncreative ass. I can feel like an artist. Through the years I've ran into some cool sites: - doom site - emo girl - motherfucking website The last one especially is this sites mantra; simplicity and efficicny. I'm trying to avoid javasctipt usage for now but who knows, maybe I won't be able to resist if the functionality is cool enough. There's only so much css can do alone. Besides a simple, lightweight, fast site, I had to think what I want this place to *actually* be about. A personal blog of sorts open to the public? I guess that's what all blogs are but I don't want it to be a diary. An educational source of hidden knowledge? Ye right, you take me for a scholar.
